Explore Satara

Nearby Attractions

Satara is blessed with natural wonders and historic marvels. Here's what awaits you just minutes from our hotel.

🌸
Kaas Plateau
📍 22 km from hotel

UNESCO World Heritage Site — a magical valley of flowers blooming in vibrant colours each monsoon season.

🏰
Sajjangad Fort
📍 14 km from hotel

Historic fort and spiritual centre — the samadhi of Saint Ramdas Swami, offering panoramic views of the valley.

💧
Thoseghar Waterfalls
📍 20 km from hotel

One of Maharashtra's tallest waterfalls, cascading dramatically through lush green valleys — a breathtaking sight.

⚔️
Ajinkyatara Fort
📍 5 km from hotel

A proud Maratha stronghold from the Shivaji era, offering commanding views of Satara city and surrounding hills.
